NUTANIX-FIRST OPERATIONS

Nutanix is the anchor. Everything around it stops feeling disconnected.

Overwatch starts where most teams actually feel the pain: Prism is critical, but hardware, networking, backup posture, and storage dependencies still live somewhere else. The product is designed to keep Nutanix at the center while pulling the surrounding operational evidence into one console.

INSIDE THE APPLIANCE

Five engines. One console. Here's what's inside.

Overwatch is not a pretty dashboard sitting on top of disconnected data. It is a local operations stack that collects, enriches, analyzes, stores, and publishes infrastructure signals from one place. That is how the product reduces console sprawl instead of adding another screen to check.

LOCAL APPLIANCE, OPTIONAL FORWARDING

Replace console sprawl without replacing the tools your team already trusts.

Overwatch runs as a local processing and storage layer that consolidates Nutanix, backup, storage, hardware, network, and flow evidence before optionally forwarding selected data to the cloud platforms your team already uses. Local first. Cloud when you choose it.
